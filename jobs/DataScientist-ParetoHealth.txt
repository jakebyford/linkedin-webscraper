Job Title:  Data Scientist Department  : Information Technology - AnalyticsAbout The TeamAt Pareto, we harness rich healthcare data and analytics to support our customers to make informed decisions around their employee health benefit offerings. Analytics is a growing team of highly skilled analytic resources who work together toward a culture of data-driven decision-making across the organization. We use an array of healthcare and internal business data to provide analytic insights that support the entire organization, including Cost Containment, Underwriting, Account Management, Product Management, and Sales and Marketing. We believe in a culture of collaboration, shared responsibility, mutual respect, and growth.Our key focus areas include: Assessing the specific impact of Pareto solutions on the cost and quality of healthcare for our member organizations  Identifying opportunities for cost savings and quality improvements for our member organizations  Providing deep intelligence on population risk for new and renewing member organizations  Designing metrics and insights that provide internal visibility into business and customer “health” and identify areas for improvement About The RoleAs a Data Scientist, you will work with a team bringing insights to life to shape our product strategy and roadmap, deepen customer insights and improve savings, develop proof points for the market, and support strategic decision-making at the company. In partnership with cross-functional stakeholders, you will help execute the vision and strategy for the Analytics team and develop data models and tools to advance our internal operations and external partnerships. This position will report to the VP of Analytics.This position requires experience working with rich, multi-faceted data that include medical and pharmacy claims as well as CRM data, plus deep skills in analytic methods and data science techniques.The Data Scientist will: Understand and champion the Analytics team’s roadmap and priorities  Lead all phases of analyses, including:  Partnering with stakeholders to ensure understanding of business priorities  Designing, testing, and refining the appropriate analytic methodologies  Building necessary data queries and visualizations to execute the solution  Presenting results in a clear and efficient manner  Appropriately documenting and maintaining the methodologies used  Partner with Product and Engineering to streamline delivery of analytic outputs  Proactively seek out opportunities where analytic insights can improve business outcomes  Promote a culture of data-driven decision making across the organization  Key Characteristics  A problem-solver with a natural sense of curiosity, an analytic mindset, and a deep love of data  A natural “leader” who takes ownership of a challenge and sees it through to completion  A collaborator with proven ability to work cross-functionally across many different stakeholders to create and deliver insights  A strong communicator who effectively shares information at the appropriate level across a wide variety of audiences  A self-starter with a demonstrated track record of leading projects from idea to execution  A critical thinker with demonstrated ability to be creative, strategic, and to apply strong business judgement  A translator who can turn complex analysis into actionable insights and recommendations for the business Specific Qualifications Master’s degree in a quantitative/analytic field or equivalent work experience  8+ years’ experience in a healthcare-based analytics role  5+ years’ experience working directly with administrative and/or clinical healthcare data (medical and pharmacy claims, EMR data is a plus); FHIR or EDI experience is a plus  Experience working with data from CRM systems, e.g., Salesforce  Experience in data cleaning, analysis, and modeling using large, complex datasets  Experience with statistical modeling techniques and common analytical frameworks at scale; familiarity with machine learning techniques and algorithms  Expert SQL skills and proficiency in programming languages such as Python, R, or Java  Experience with data science tools and platforms like Jupyter, Pandas, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, or similar  Strong data visualization skills using tools such as Tableau  Strong understanding of the healthcare insurance industry  Bonus: Familiarity with or experience supporting underwriting and
