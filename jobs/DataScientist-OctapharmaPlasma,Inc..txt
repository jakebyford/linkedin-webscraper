Position title: Data Scientist

Company: Octapharma Plasma, Inc.


ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:Use advanced data modeling and analysis techniques to distill insights that will guide strategic decisions and uncover optimization opportunities.Transform data science prototypes and apply machine learning algorithms and tools to automate predictive models.Communicate the business value and champion analytics as a central decision-making resource.Architect and work closely with other team members to develop scalable data analytic solutions.Apply the latest technologies and methodologies in machine learning, data mining, and predictive analytics to correlate disparate datasets and events, and derive value.Identify valuable data sources and automate mining/collection processes.Undertake preprocessing of structured and unstructured data.Analyze large amounts of data/information to discover and articulate trends and patterns.Build predictive models and machine-learning algorithms.Combine models through ensemble modeling.Develop conclusive insights using statistical methods.Make and defend arguments using data and data-based insight.Gather business requirements from end users and oversee the build of technical solutions from concept to implementation.Leverage a variety of data mining/data analysis methods in order to test and propose effectiveness of different courses of action.Assess the effectiveness and accuracy of new data Computer programming / Data engineering Experience working with enterprise data warehouses, building data pipelines, and feature engineering.Working knowledge of multiple programming languages and statistical packages, as well as knowledge of dataset tools and Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning (AI/ML) tools (preferred: Azure).Experience using statistical computer languages to manipulate data and draw insights from large data sets (required: SQL, R or Python; preferred: Spark, SAS, Julia, SPSS).Preferable experience implementing machine learning tools such as Azure from the ground up.Preferable experience with CRM software (preferably Salesforce). Data visualization Experience with data visualization software (Tableau, Power BI, MicroStrategy, Qlik, think-cell, etc.) and distilling data into meaningful graphics. Individual skills Excellent communication and presentation skills; ability to communicate technical results to non-technical audience.Experience supporting digital transformation of workflows and business processes.Strategic mindset with strong organizational, analytical, quality detail orientation, negotiation, decision-making, time management, and process improvement skills.Works well in a collaborative, team-oriented environment.Ability to work autonomously with very limited supervision.
