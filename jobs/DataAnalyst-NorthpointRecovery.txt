Job Title: Data AnalystReports to: Vice President, Clinical ServicesNorthpoint Recovery Holdings, LLC, established in 2009, is a growing, behavioral healthcare treatment platform treating adults with substance use disorder and co-occurring conditions under the Northpoint Recovery brand and adolescents with mental healthcare conditions under the Imagine by Northpoint brand. Northpoint has grown from two facilities to fifteen today entirely on a de novo basis with plans to expand rapidly in both existing and new markets. Organizational values include humility, heart, inspiration and conviction. Our commitment to excellence means doing good for others and engaging in innovative empirical based treatment. In short, our mission is to help people get their lives back and show them respect and empathy in the process.POSITION SUMMARY: As a key member of the Northpoint team, the Data Analyst (Outcomes) is responsible for gathering, compiling, analyzing, validating, managing, and reviewing current and proposed data sets and stakeholder needs via EMR, financial statements, KPIs and other key metrics, and reporting that information in a useful means to aid decision making. This person is specifically assigned to the Outcomes Program for Northpoint, charged with measuring and analyzing outcomes for patients post-discharge. This person will identify trends and opportunities for growth through analysis of complex data sets including financial, clinical and medical data. This role will pull data from our electronic medical record (EMR), general ledger software, customer relationship management (CRM) and call center software to assess overall effectiveness, and devise solutions to problems.
