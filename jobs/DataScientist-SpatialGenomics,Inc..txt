Position title: Data Scientist

Company: Spatial Genomics, Inc.


Join the frontiers of spatial biology research at Spatial Genomics! At Spatial Genomics, we are on a mission to develop revolutionary platform technologies for diverse applications of spatial genomic analysis of human and other biology. Be a part of our mission to enable new discoveries in basic research and healthcare. We are seeking exceptional data scientists to join our passionate team and contribute to groundbreaking discoveries.  Description:As a Data Scientist at Spatial Genomics, you will have the opportunity to work with cutting-edge technologies and diverse datasets, including images and single-cell data. Your role will be pivotal in extracting valuable insights and driving discoveries through advanced data analysis. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams alongside members of R&D, Manufacturing, Marketing, Operations, and your biology, bioinformatics, & software engineering colleagues. At Spatial Genomics, we thrive in a dynamic and rapidly evolving environment. We value proactive and independent thinkers who can execute priorities efficiently, even with limited resources, support, and high expectations.  Key Responsibilities:Unleash the Power of Biological Data: Derive valuable insights from complex biological data using advanced analysis techniques.Pioneer New Analytical Tools: Develop cutting-edge algorithms and tools to analyze imaging data, pushing the boundaries of what is possible.Shape the Data Analysis Landscape: Create robust pipelines for efficient data analysis, allowing for streamlined interpretation and visualization.Illuminate the Biological World: Design methods and visualizations that shed light on spatial relationships within biological systems.Empower Scientific Exploration: Assist in data extraction from spatial images, enabling researchers to delve deeper into their investigations.Stay at the Forefront of Research: Continuously review and validate methods published in scientific literature, keeping abreast of the latest advancements.Document and Share Knowledge: Accurately document code and processes, contributing to the collective learning of the team.Collaborate and Inspire: Support and mentor others in utilizing software packages and pipelines, fostering a culture of collaboration and growth.Drive Innovation: Contribute significantly to achieving our overall product development objectives.Embrace Challenges: Embrace new challenges and take on additional duties as assigned, always striving for excellence. Required Qualifications:Advanced Scientific Background: PhD or MS in a Science discipline, Computer Science, or a related field.Research Experience: Prior experience in scientific research, demonstrating your strong foundation in the scientific method.Analytical Prowess: Proficiency in quantitative analysis, including microscopy image analysis, algorithm development, and large-scale quantitative analysis.Coding Expertise: Strong coding skills with working knowledge of Python and C++, enabling you to create innovative solutions.Machine Learning Experience: Experience in machine learning techniques, empowering you to unlock patterns and insights in complex datasets.Statistical Fluency: Sound understanding of statistics and linear algebra, providing the necessary statistical rigor in your analyses.Technical Dexterity: Familiarity with version control systems like Git and GitHub, ensuring efficient collaboration and code management.Agility and Adaptability: Ability to quickly learn and apply new tools and technologies, embracing the ever-evolving nature of genomics research.Passion for Collaboration: Interest in the life sciences and enthusiasm for collaborating with life science researchers, fostering a multidisciplinary approach.Preferred Qualifications:Biological Research Experience: Prior involvement in biological research, allowing you to bring domain-specific knowledge to the team.Tech-Savvy Mindset: Prior experience in relevant software development or genomics research, equipping you with a broader perspective on technological advancements.Proficiency with Advanced Tools: Familiarity with digital pathology applications or cloud-based analysis tools/platforms, demonstrating your ability to effectively utilize advanced tools in your work.
